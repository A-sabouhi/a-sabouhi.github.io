    A Developer’s Walkthrough & Feature Highlights


This sample project is a custom-built layout inspired by the official MSI website. As a front-end developer, I aimed to replicate and reinterpret many of the impressive design and technical elements found on their landing page. The site is developed entirely with HTML and CSS, and most sections also include JavaScript to enhance interactivity and functionality.


         diamond Key Features & Highlights:


check_circle Fully Responsive Design
Each section adapts seamlessly to various screen sizes, with custom adjustments made to optimize layout and usability on smaller devices.

check_circle Advanced Navigation Menu
A complex and dynamic navigation bar offers an intuitive user experience across all viewports.

check_circle Hero Section Slider
An automatic, landscape-style image slider adds motion and engagement right from the first glance.

check_circle Custom Cookie Settings Widget
A triangular menu in the lower-left corner acts as a functional placeholder for cookie settings or can be easily adapted into a quick-access menu or mini-page.

check_circle Sticky Floating Bubble Animation
A visually engaging animated bubble remains fixed on-screen as users scroll through the page.

check_circle Interactive Hover Effects
Almost every element on the site features unique hover animations, enhancing the overall user experience with subtle visual feedback.
